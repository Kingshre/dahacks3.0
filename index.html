Space waste management

Space waste management focuses on tracking, reducing, and removing debris from orbit to protect satellites and spacecraft. With increasing satellite launches, the risk of collisions grows, threatening the safety of missions and the sustainability of space. Effective management is essential to prevent a debris cascade that could make certain orbits unusable, ensuring the long-term safety of space exploration and preserving a clear path for future missions.
